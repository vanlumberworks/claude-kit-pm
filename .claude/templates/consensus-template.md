## Usage Notes

**When to use this template**:
- High-stakes decisions requiring alignment
- Decisions with significant stakeholder disagreement
- Complex trade-offs requiring negotiation
- Strategic decisions affecting multiple teams

**How to complete**:
1. Fill out context and initial positions during/after stakeholder interviews
2. Document evidence before consensus session
3. Update positions and discussion during consensus meetings
4. Complete decision analysis after reaching agreement
5. Define clear action items and owners
6. Set up monitoring and review schedule

**Tips for success**:
- Be transparent with all evidence
- Document dissenting views respectfully
- Focus on interests, not positions
- Make trade-offs explicit
- Ensure commitments are specific and owned
- Follow up consistently on action items
